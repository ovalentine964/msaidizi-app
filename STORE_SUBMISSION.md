## Full Description (EN)
Msaidizi is the AI CFO that every informal worker deserves but could never afford. It's not an assistant — it's a proactive business partner that speaks your language, tracks your money, and helps you build wealth.

🎤 VOICE-FIRST — Speak in your language (Swahili, Sheng, Kikuyu, Dholuo, Luhya, Kalenjin, Maasai, Somali, Amharic, Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Zulu, Xhosa)

📴 OFFLINE-FIRST — Works without internet. Syncs when connected.

💰 M-PESA AUTO-CAPTURE — Automatically tracks M-Pesa transactions from SMS.

📊 BUSINESS FLOW — M-Pesa-style cash flow visualization. See your money move.

🎮 GAMIFICATION — Earn points, levels, streaks, and badges for healthy financial habits.

🧠 AI CFO — Daily briefings, cash flow forecasting, credit readiness scoring.

💰 WEALTH MINDSET — Track 10 daily "Rich Habits" inspired by financial best practices.

🤲 TITHE & GIVING — Track tithes and charitable giving.

🎯 GOALS & LOANS — Set savings goals and track loan repayments.

🔒 BANK-GRADE SECURITY — AES-256 encryption, post-quantum cryptography ready.

Built by Angavu Intelligence Ltd — making Africa's 600M+ invisible workers visible through data.
