# Msaidizi Roadmap

## Vision

To empower small business owners in Kenya with an AI-powered business assistant that makes tracking sales, profits, and inventory as easy as having a conversation.

## Current Status

### ✅ Completed (v1.0)

- **Onboarding Flow**
  - Introduction phase (name exchange)
  - Business discovery phase
  - WhatsApp connection phase ← NEW
  - Personality and preferences
  - First use tutorial

- **WhatsApp Integration**
  - Phone validation (Kenyan numbers)
  - Verification flow
  - Report delivery

  - Multi-language support

  - Report generation
